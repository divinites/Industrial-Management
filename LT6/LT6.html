denote:
$p_s$ as the probablity that your rival chooses Straightforward;
1-$p_s$ as the probability that your rival chooses Avoid

Choosing S
$\pi_s = p_s * -20 + (1-p_s)* 5 = 5-25p_s$
Choosing A:


$\pi_a = 0 * (1-p_s) + -10 * p_s = -10p_s$
If $\pi_a > \pi_s$, choose Avoid; otherwise choose Straightforward

$\pi_a > \pi_s => -10p_s > 5-25p_s => p_s > \frac{1}{3}$.
