Profit Maximizing Principle
 Imagine that you are a manager, 
 would it be optimal to stop producing 
when MR>MC? 

NO!
If MR>MC, Selling additional products always profitable,
why not producing more?

 Imagine that you are a manager, 
 would it be optimal to stop producing 
when MR<MC? 

NO!
If MR<MC, Selling additional products always
leads to a loss, why producing then?
